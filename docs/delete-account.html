Delete Account & Data

Zen Word Wizard allows users to permanently delete their account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly inside the app by following these steps:

  1. Open Zen Word Wizard
  2. Go to the Home Screen
  3. Open the side menu
  4. Select Settings
  5. Tap Delete Account
Important: Deleting your account will permanently remove your authentication record and all stored game progress. This action is irreversible.
